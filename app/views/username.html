$def with (username='',question=None,game_over=False,game_complete=False,question_number=0,level=0,score=0) $var title: quiz

Enter your name

 


Rules:

  1. You start with 3 lives.
  2. Each question has 3 multiple choice answers (Busted, Plausible, Confirmed).
  3. Each question answered right adds 2 points to your score.
  4. Each question answered wrong deducts 1 life from your total Lives.
  5. You have a time limit to answer each question, or it counts wrong.
  6. Initial time limit is 20 seconds; every 3 levels, time limit is decreased by 1 second.
  7. Every 3 levels, you win an extra life (Your lives can be max 5).
  8. For each level passed, you get a bonus question
  9. Each bonus question is composed by several subquestions.
  10. Each subquestions answered right adds 1 to bonus multiplier.
  11. Bonus question ends when you answer wrong or when time limit is over.
  12. When bonus question ends, gained multiplier will be applied to your score.
  13. Enjoy the quiz.
